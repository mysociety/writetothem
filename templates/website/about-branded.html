WriteToThem for your website

If you just want to link to WriteToThem to tell other people about it, that’s easy. But maybe you want something shinier than that – you’ve designed a brand new website for your campaign, you want people to write to their MPs to encourage them to support it, and you’d absolutely love this to happen without people needing to leave your website at all.

Great news! Someone has written a little application that allows you to do this. It’s called WriteToMP, and here’s an example of it in action at the Lighter Later campaign. You’ll see that it looks exactly like the campaign’s website – it uses WriteToThem to send the message without the user ever needing to know that that’s what’s going on.

Before you start, make sure you read our guidelines for campaigners. You must not tell your users to send cut-and-paste messages – they are against our conditions of use and we will block them. The great thing about this application is that it allows you to suggest the points that you’d like people to make when they write their message, but also stresses that they must write in their own words and explains why this is important.

Ready to have a go? Here’s where you can get the code. Make sure you read all the instructions on that page – it should be fairly self-explanatory and you don’t need much technical knowledge to get it working.

If you have any problems installing the code, contact us and we’ll give you a hand.
