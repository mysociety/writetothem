
## We want to help you get your issue to the right person

As someone living in the UK, you have many representatives at different layers of government. 

Different representatives have different responsibilities. Sometimes you have multiple representatives at the same level. 

This page explains the responsibilities of the different types of representative to help you make your choice.

Your representatives might also be able to help you with problems outside their area of responsibility, but this is best as an escalation after other routes have been tried.
