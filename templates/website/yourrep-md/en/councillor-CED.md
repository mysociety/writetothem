# When should I contact a County Councillor?

County Councils are one of two tiers of local government in some parts of England. Your County Councillor can help with issues handled by the county council, including:

- **Education** and schools
- **Transport and roads**, including highways maintenance and road safety
- **Fire and rescue** services
- **Social services** and adult social care
- **Libraries**
- **Trading standards** and consumer protection

For issues such as council housing, planning applications, rubbish collection, and the local environment, you should contact your District Councillor instead.

Don't worry if you're not absolutely sure who to pick! Whoever you write to should forward your letter to the most appropriate person, if you haven't got it quite right.
