# When should I contact a London Borough Councillor?

London Boroughs are responsible for most local government services in your area. Your London Borough Councillor can help with issues including:

- **Housing** and homelessness
- **Planning applications** and local development
- **Education** and schools
- **Social services** and adult social care
- **Rubbish collection** and recycling
- **Roads and parking** (except for major roads managed by Transport for London)
- **Council tax** collection and benefits
- **Libraries**, leisure and parks
- **Environmental health** and noise complaints

For London-wide strategic issues such as transport policy, policing, and fire services, you should contact your London Assembly Member instead.

Don't worry if you're not absolutely sure who to pick! Whoever you write to should forward your letter to the most appropriate person, if you haven't got it quite right.
